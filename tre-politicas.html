Standard Cancellation Policy
This policy applies exclusively to bookings made at standard published rates. Discounted rates, group bookings, and special offers are subject to separate conditions.

Cancellation Deadline:
  • Cancellations made more than 48 hours before check-in: Free of charge
  • Cancellations made within 48 hours of check-in or no-shows: 100% of the booking amount will be charged

Accepted Cancellation Methods:
  • Phone (during business hours)
  • Email to the official reservations address
  • Via the original booking platform
  • In person at reception

Refund Terms:
  • On-time cancellation: Full refund (100% of booking + taxes) via original payment method
  • Late cancellation/no-show: Non-refundable charge of 100% of booking amount

Refund Processing: Refunds will be processed within 7-14 business days, subject to banking institution timelines.

Confirmation: Written cancellation confirmation will be provided within 24 hours of request.


Policy Exclusions:
  • Promotional or discounted rates
  • Group reservations with special terms
  • Third-party bookings governed by their own policies
  • Specific agreements documented at time of booking

Guest Responsibilities:
  • Must use authorized cancellation channels only
  • The establishment is not liable for:
    • Incorrect contact information provided
    • Technical failures in communication systems
    • Messages received outside business hours
    • No-shows without prior cancellation
